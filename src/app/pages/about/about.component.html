Our History

Founded in 2011, Pantheon Law has been a provider of legal services for nearly ten years. Pantheon’s lawyers and staff have worked across practices, jurisdictions to provide simple, effectively approaches to their most complex legal and business challenges to clients, whether domestic or international. We always support every client relationship with intellectual agility as well as commercial acumen.

OUR CAPABILITIES

Today, Pantheon Law has provided a full range of services to domestic and international clients who conduct investing and business in Vietnam. Specially, Pantheon’s lawyers regularly advise clients globally on corporate governance, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), restructuring, real estate, housing, etc. in Vietnam.<br/> We have successfully consulted to developers or promoters of projects as well as traders, investors, landowners, contractors, operators, and suppliers. We frequently represent clients involved in asset, project or company acquisitions. Our lawyers has extensive experiences of representing large institutional investors such as finance investors, real estate investors, foreign investors conducting business investment in Vietnam, in connection with their investments into or acquisitions of infrastructure and assets.

OUR CLIENTS

Our lawyers have represented some of the financial corporations, asset managers, real estate investment and developers, including: An Gia Investment; Southern General Import-Export JSC; Atrix Capital Viet Nam Investment Consulting Co., LTD etc. <br/>In 2015, Pantheon Law became the legal representative of Kova Trading JSC – one of the leading paint brands in Vietnam.
